Welcome to Pintereach

Pintereach is an online platform designed from the ground up to ease the life of todays academic. Keep all of you most commonly used articles and papers organized and ready for citations. Organize by category and use our card view to select by cover page.

The Future of Pintereach

Future versions of the Pintereach App will include many Quality of Life improvements. Add friends and colleagues to share and collaborate. Integrate your social media accounts to find and collaborate quickly with a more diverse group of candidates.

Organization

Our system allows you to assign your saved articles to categories. Keep your articles seperated by discipline or sub-discipline for easy referance.

Speed

Keep all of your articles at hand and ready for use. Make the citation process faster by getting rid of all the binders on your book-shelf.

Efficiency

Spend less time putting together your references and more time getting published with the power of Pintereach
